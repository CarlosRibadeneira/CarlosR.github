About me:

My name is Carlos Ribadeneira and I am a data analyst. I have been trained to understand business complexities, identify problems, devise solutions and seek opportunities. Since I started my education as a Business Engineer, I began to look at the world from a different perspective. I joined a research training group and shortly after I took my first steps as a researcher. As I was walking in this rewarding journey, I was part of various projects where I was fortunate enough to explore different business problems, understand and clean their data, as well as work closely with a team to develop models. As part of my research group, we addressed projects such as developing methodology to identify IT processes, propose sustainable business models with circular economy principles, enhance financial sustainability for electric and electronic waste in a city and evaluate KPI of local governments. Working in academia was the perfect way to integrate research with the public sector. These experiences impelled me to continue my education and focus on my passion “business decisions driven by data,” but this time in the private sector. I graduated with a Master’s of Science in Business Analytics. Meanwhile, I have been working as a market analyst. I have been able to apply my new set of skills and strengthen my old ones. Working towards becoming a better data analyst has taught me that value generation and continuous improvement are directly proportional to constant learning, discipline, honesty, hard work and of course, meaningful data leading to actionable steps. I am excited to present a few projects that helped me consolidate my technical knowledge.
